I am an engineer working within the public sector at the Internal Resources Division, Ministry of Finance, Bangladesh. My work sits at the intersection of infrastructure, governance, and the quiet machinery that keeps institutions running.

Beyond my formal role, I am deeply interested in building things — products, systems, and ideas that matter. I believe in the compounding value of curiosity, and in the rare kind of work that makes both an institution and a person better.

Based in Dhaka, Bangladesh
